Injury Reserve:

To respect the specificity of all three of us as Injury Reserve, we have decided not to make new music under this name.
Nathaniel and Parker have continued working together and plan to release under By Storm as a hand off from our work within Injury Reserve.
We have produced a limited 5-LP box set of all official Injury Reserve releases as a trio alongside individual pressings of Floss and Live From the Dentist Office available here.
